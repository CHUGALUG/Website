Here's a post from Glenn Leavell...

Subject: ALF '99 v 2.0

Excited? We are also! Keith Ledford and I are looking for volunteers to help plan the next Athens Linux Fest. We are forming a planning committee to brainstorm ideas. E-mail either Keith Ledford or myself if you are interested in helping to form ALF '99 version 2.0. We will be meeting on March 18th at 7:00pm. Location to be announced. We will then present our ideas at the next CHUGALUG meeting on March 25. If the group accepts our proposal, we will then look for volunteers to produce the plan.

(2/25 1999) MEETING TONIGHT! It's time again for another fabulous CHUGALUG meeting! Tonight's meeting will have a game focus. Here's the mini-presentations that are planned:

Coding a MUD on Linux using Java
Running a QuakeII server on Linux
Demo of QuakeII and/or BFRIS on Linux
Running an Ultima Off-line server on Linux (canceled)

Other things you might hear talked about.. cable modems, Linux 2.2.2, Linux Mandrake, UGA English department switching to Linux and more!

O'Reilly & Associates sent us a copy of the "Linux Multimedia Guide" and we will be giving it away at tonight meeting. We will also be giving a way a full copy for BRU2000 backup software donated by BRU themselves.

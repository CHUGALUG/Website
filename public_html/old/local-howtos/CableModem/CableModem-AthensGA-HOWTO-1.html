1. Purpose

The Cable Modem Athens,GA HOWTO is intended to serve as a guide for people who need help configuring their personal Linux box for use the the @home internet service offered by Intermedia in Athens, GA.

1.1 Other sources of information

  • If you want to learn more about Linux, the Linux Documentation Projectis a good place to start.
  • Cable Modem mini-howto.. yes there's another more general one in the LDP.
  • IP Masquerade mini-HOWTO
  • aah@giffords.net and aah-security@giffords.net are two listserves for people using @home in Athens, GA. They aren't Linux specific, but you'll find no shortage of Linux info either.

    To subscribe send mail to majordomo@giffords.net with the body of your message reading:

    subscribe aah

    or

    subscribe aah-security

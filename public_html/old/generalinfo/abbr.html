The name CHUGALUG

The letters "C H U G A L U G" stand for Classic Hackers UGA Linux Users Group. This name was originally posed by Hal Brooks one of the earliest members of CHUGALUG. It pays homage to the fact that the father of Linux, Linus Torvalds, has an affection for beer.

Drinking or a love of beer are not required to
a) run Linux or b) attend CHUGALUG meetings

Please don't be put off by a name... it's only a name.

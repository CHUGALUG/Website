ugalinux@listserv.uga.edu
Serves as an e-mail bi-directional gateway to uga.tech.linux for people who either can't get the uga.* newsgroups or who prefer to particpate via e-mail instead of through news. In order to keep the mailing list reserved for local Linux users, subscription is moderated. Send e-mail to ugalinux-request@listserv.uga.edu and ask to be put on the list.

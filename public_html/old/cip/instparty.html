Registration Form - Linux InstallFest

Please fill out this form completely. If you don't, evil things will happy to your installation! It lets us know what kind of hardware is coming.

Filling out this form will force you to become intimate with your machine. You will know a lot more about your machine after answering the following questions. It will help you when you need to compile your kernel, or want to add some hardware. And most of all, it will help out if you have any problems during the install.

You might want to look at Red Hat Hardware Compatibility Lists if you have questions about the suitabilty of your hardware.

This form was inspired by and mostly copied from a similar form created by the Washington D.C. Linux Users Group.
